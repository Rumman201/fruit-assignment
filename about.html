Our story

From Local Farms To City Homes

We started by helping a few neighborhoods get access to reliably fresh produce in smaller, cleaner batches. As customer demand grew, we built a sourcing network that prioritizes farm transparency, predictable ripening windows, and rapid dispatch.

Today, Fruit Burst supports households, cafes, and offices with tailored fruit boxes designed around seasonality, value, and consistent quality.

Our process

How We Keep Fruit Market-Fresh

  1. Pre-harvest forecasting helps us source only what can be delivered in peak condition.
  2. Inbound quality checks flag bruising, over-ripeness, and temperature variance.
  3. Smart eco-packaging balances airflow and moisture for transport stability.
  4. Dispatch windows are optimized by delivery cluster to reduce travel time.
