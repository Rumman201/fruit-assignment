    Delivery and Ordering FAQ

    Can I choose a fixed delivery time?

    Yes. During checkout you can choose available windows. Premium slots are prioritized by route load.

    What if I miss my delivery?

    Our rider will attempt a callback. If unreachable, we reattempt once and then move to next slot.

    Do you offer cash on delivery?

    Yes, cash on delivery is supported in most city zones alongside mobile and card payments.

    Can I pause a subscription box?

    Yes. Subscription plans can be paused or shifted from your account dashboard with 24-hour notice.
